EXP LANE

Sustained Frontliners & Flankers

The EXP Lane requires incredible resilience and independent decision-making. As an EXP Laner, your job is to secure your lane early, survive solo pressure, and level up quickly to impact the first Turtle fight.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Wave Freezing: Deny the enemy EXP laner gold and experience by holding the minion wave near your turret.
  • Flanking Targets: In team fights, bypass the enemy tank and dive straight onto the backline Gold Lane marksman or Mage.
  • Split Pushing: Create map pressure on the opposite side of active objectives to force enemy rotations.
GOLD LANE

Late-Game Primary Marksman Carry

The Gold Lane is all about patience, scaling, and flawless positioning. You start the game weak, but your ultimate goal is to farm safely until you acquire your core damage items to carry the late-game team fights.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Safe Farming: Prioritize last-hitting minions and avoiding early deaths over chasing aggressive kills.
  • Positioning: Always stand behind your Tank and Fighter during fights. Never face-check bushes blindly.
  • Item Countering: Learn to build defensive items early (like Wind of Nature or Athena's Shield) to survive assassin bursts.
JUNGLER

Objective Security & Core Rotation

The Jungler controls the pacing of the match. Armed with the Retribution spell, you are responsible for maintaining camp rotation efficiency, executing map ganks, and securing major neutral objectives.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Retri Timing: Master tracking objective health bars to secure Turtles and Lords under intense enemy pressure.
  • Rotation Paths: Optimize your jungle clear speed to arrive at the side lanes exactly when minion waves collide for a surprise gank.
  • Buff Management: Protect your Purple and Red buffs from being invaded and stolen by the enemy roamer.
MID LANE

High Magic Burst & High Map Control

The Mid Lane acts as the central pivot point of the map. As a Mage, your primary duty is to clear your minion waves quickly so you can rotate with the Jungler and Roamer to provide magical burst damage during team fights.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Fast Clear: Melt the early mid minion waves instantly to gain immediate rotation priority over the enemy Mage.
  • Bush Check: Use long-range skills to check dark bushes safely before letting your squishier teammates pass through.
  • Burst Damage: Target clumped enemy groups or backline squishies to thin out their health before objective clashes begin.
ROAMER

Map Vision & Team Protection

The Roamer operates without minion lane gold, relying on map presence, dynamic crowd control (CC), and setups. Whether you play a beefy Tank, an aggressive Support, or a hidden Assassin, you provide the vision your team needs to survive.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Vision Control: Anchor yourself in high-traffic river bushes to track the enemy Jungler's movements and pathing.
  • Initiating Setups: Use your primary crowd control abilities to lock down high-value targets or peel aggressive assassins off your carries.
  • Item Utility: Buy items like Dominance Ice, Radiant Armor, or Flask of the Oasis depending entirely on the enemy's damage profile.
