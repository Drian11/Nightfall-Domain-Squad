Drafting Strategy

Win matches before loading onto the tactical battlefield by mastering phase planning.

Counter-Picking System

Analyze opponent team compositions on the fly to systematically deny high-priority meta choices and secure field advantages.

  • ◈ Track enemy comfort heroes early.
  • ◈ Save your Roam/EXP picks for late draft.
  • ◈ Balance physical vs magic damage types.

S-Tier Compositions

Synchronize ultimate hero abilities to build unstoppable early-game dive setups or scalable late-game siege defenses.

  • ◈ Wombo-Combo ultimate synergies.
  • ◈ Match your Jungler's pacing speed.
  • ◈ Establish high map pressure flex picks.

Targeted Ban Priority

Identify and block over-tuned meta choices or signature picks that destroy your planned team fight compositions.

  • ◈ Remove high flex-rate S-tier options.
  • ◈ Target counter-picks against your carry.
  • ◈ Force the enemy into unfamiliar hero tiers.
